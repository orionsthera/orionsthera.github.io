About My Art

Welcome to my official fine art gallery. I’m Dan Sproul — a photographer and mixed media artist specializing in nature photography, vintage patent designs, and landscape art. My work blends scenic photography from locations such as Colorado, Alaska, and Ohio with unique artistic overlays, bringing history and nature together in one frame.
