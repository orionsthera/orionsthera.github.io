About Dan Sproul

Dan Sproul is an award-winning photographer and artist based in Lima, Ohio, with over ten years of experience capturing the beauty of the natural world, historic landmarks, and creative mixed media art. His work spans wildlife, vibrant landscapes, and vintage-inspired designs, offering collectors high-quality fine art prints that bring timeless beauty into homes and galleries worldwide. From the rugged peaks of Colorado to the serene waters of Alaska, Dan’s photography and mixed media compositions are crafted to inspire and captivate art enthusiasts everywhere.
